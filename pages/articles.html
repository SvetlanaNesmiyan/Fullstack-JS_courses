Wuthering Waves: The New Open-World RPG

Published on June 12, 2025

Wuthering Waves is Kuro Games' highly anticipated open-world action RPG featuring stunning visuals and a unique resonance combat system. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, players control "Rovers" who can absorb enemies' abilities. The game emphasizes fluid movement and combo-based combat, with a gacha system for obtaining characters.

Key Features:

  • Massive open world with vertical exploration
  • Echo System for capturing enemy abilities
  • Dynamic weather and day-night cycle
  • Co-op multiplayer support
